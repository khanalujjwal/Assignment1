Changunarayan

Hiking

A hike to Nepal's Changunarayan temple is a once-in-a-lifetime adventure. This historic temple, one of the oldest and most renowned in Nepal, is situated on a hill in the Kathmandu valley. Moreover, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, drawing tourists from all over the globe.Although it's a short climb, the Changunarayan temple gives breathtaking views of the valleys and hills in the area. The well-maintained trail travels past terraced farms, traditional Nepalese villages, and lush forests. You will come across a number of tiny temples and shrines along the ascent, each with its own special beauty and background.

Jamacho

Hiking

When visiting Kathmandu, Nepal, outdoor enthusiasts frequently go on hikes to Jamacho. A hill called Jamacho, also called Jamachowk, is situated in the Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park not far from the city. The hill provides a sweeping perspective of the city and the nearby Himalayan mountain ranges.Due to its steep ascent and uneven terrain, the hike to Jamacho is rated moderate to hard. The trail winds its way through lush oak and rhododendron forests from the national park's entrance. You will come across a number of small stupas and shrines as you hike up.
